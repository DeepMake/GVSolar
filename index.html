Goal

GV SOLAR is a company whose goal is to offer rooftop-based solar power, with all of its benefits, to companies that otherwise would be unable to generate it.

Operation

In order to make this happen, we install, operate, and maintain solar panels on rooftops, making it possible for companies, office buildings, schools, commercial complexes, and medical facilities to generate solar power without financial or operational risk.

Join Us

Customers can become part of the government’s new initiative to generate more solar power by offering up their unused space – with no cost to themselves. In going green, they can lower part of their electricity bill (relative to the utility’s electricity rates).

New Technology

GV SOLAR uses the newest technology, optimizing each site’s potential with long-lasting, tested components.

Quality

GV SOLAR uses the highest quality technologies and equipment to ensure that the project is beneficial to you in the long term. Longevity is important for us, and we select all our components with this goal in mind.
